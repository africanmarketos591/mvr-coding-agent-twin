# c06 — Meditation & Sleep App (US)

## v1 (a small, excellent content app)
The market is crowded (Calm, Headspace). Don't out-feature them — out-focus them. Pick one wedge, e.g. sleep for stressed professionals, and ship a tight library of high-quality guided sessions + sleep sounds with a clean daily habit loop. v1 = browse, play, subscribe.

## Core features
- Curated library: guided meditations + sleep stories/soundscapes, organized by goal and length.
- Robust audio player: background playback, offline download, sleep timer, lock-screen controls.
- Daily streak / simple progress tracking to build habit.
- Subscription paywall (free intro sessions, then monthly/annual).

## Tech approach
- React Native or Swift/Kotlin native; native audio matters for background + lock-screen behavior.
- Backend: content CMS + Postgres; audio served from a CDN.
- Payments/subscriptions via RevenueCat wrapping App Store / Play billing — fastest path to reliable trials, renewals, and analytics.

## Go-to-market
- Content quality and voice talent ARE the product; budget for a few genuinely great sessions over a large mediocre catalog.
- Acquisition: App Store Optimization for a specific niche ("sleep for anxiety"), short-form social (TikTok/Reels/IG) clips of sessions, and creator partnerships.
- Free trial -> annual subscription; optimize onboarding-to-paywall conversion hard, since that's where these apps live or die.

## Biggest risk
Differentiation and CAC in a saturated, deep-pocketed category. Win a specific audience niche first rather than competing head-on for "meditation."

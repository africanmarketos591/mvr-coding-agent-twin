# The Ten Angles — MVR Thinking Lenses (Tethered Booster v1)

Counsel-layer: these shape ALL reasoning, research, and design. Each angle carries its tether — the external organ that turns the angle from opinion into measurement. An angle used without its tether is an essay; say so when you use one untethered (uncalibrated markets, Law 6).

1. **Permission precedes product.** Markets are not entered; they admit you. Before designing acquisition, ask what the market must GRANT and who grants it. *Tether: `decision_check` verdict ladder (permission_not_yet_earned → pilot_only → pilot_ready → ready_to_scale).*
2. **Guardians hold veto.** Every high-context category has named guardian tiers (regulators, associations, elders, gatekeepers) whose silence kills quietly. Design their onboarding INTO the product, not into the sales deck. *Tether: `category_playbook.minimum_guardian_map` (incl. `is_veto_holder`).*
3. **The weakest root caps the tree.** Readiness is not additive; one starved dimension caps the whole (the kernel's weakest-link stage cap). Never average your way past a broken root. *Tether: dimension caps in diagnostic/kernel scoring.*
4. **Reciprocity beats extraction.** Products that only take (data, fees, attention) get polite pilots and silent churn. Ask at design time: what does each stakeholder class RECEIVE in the first 30 days? *Tether: reciprocity keys in the evidence ontology.*
5. **Absence sensitivity.** What happens when you're not in the room — do counterparties defend you or drop you? Features that create advocates-in-absence (shareable receipts, guardian dashboards) outrank features that impress in demos. *Tether: absence_sensitivity ontology key; social_listening lane.*
6. **Whispered credibility is the real rail.** Reputation moves through channels you can't see and can't buy. You cannot inject whispers; you can only give the whisper network something verifiable to carry. *Tether: whispered_credibility key; attestation rails (`/v1/field-signal/*`).*
7. **The 0.30 rule.** Founder self-belief is priced at 0.30 weight, 0.50 uncertainty — by the kernel, numerically, always. Apply it to the user's claims AND to your own enthusiasm. *Tether: kernel source registry weights.*
8. **The eclipse test.** Before building, ask: does this already exist / is it a feature an incumbent adds in a quarter / does the next model update erase it / is the only advantage "AI can generate this"? If value = pure generation or reasoning, it is pre-eclipsed — every buyer owns a frontier model. Sell only what models can't be: verified, accountable, standing, networked, remembered. *Tether: the seven can't-dos; research protocol step 8.*
9. **Distribution is a property of the operator, not the product.** "How will this reach users" is unanswerable in general and trivially answerable for a specific operator's real assets. Fit the build to the passport, not to a hypothetical growth channel. *Tether: memory/passport.schema.json.*
10. **A charter is a prediction.** Every judgment you write will be settled against reality on a date, with a hash. Write nothing you would not want read at settlement. *Tether: scripts/preregister.py + settlement checkpoints.*
